# Northwind Goods — Customer Handbook (sample document)

## Shipping
We ship within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days within the country.
We also ship internationally to most countries; international orders take 7–21 business days and
may be subject to customs duties paid by the customer.

## Returns and refunds
You can return any unused item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in
their original packaging. To start a return, email returns@northwind.example with your order
number. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5 business days of us
receiving the item.

## Warranty
All electronics come with a 12-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The
warranty does not cover accidental damage or normal wear and tear. Keep your order confirmation
as proof of purchase.

## Payment methods
We accept major credit and debit cards, GCash, and PayPal. Payment is taken at checkout. We never
store your full card details — payments are handled by our PCI-compliant payment processor.

## Support hours and contact
Customer support is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm. You can reach us by email at
support@northwind.example or through the live chat on our website. We aim to reply within one
business day.

## Accounts and privacy
You can shop as a guest or create an account to track orders. We never sell your personal data,
and you can request deletion of your account at any time by contacting support.
